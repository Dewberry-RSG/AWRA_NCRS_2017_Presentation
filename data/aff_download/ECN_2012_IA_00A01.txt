IA1200A01
Island Areas: Geographic Area Series: Comparative Statistics by Kind of Business for American Samoa, Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, Guam, Puerto Rico, and U.S. Virgin Islands: 2012 and 2007

Release Date : 09/29/2015

Note: Includes only establishments or firms with payroll. Data based on the 2012 and 2007 Economic Census of Island Areas. Figures may not add due to rounding. For information on confidentiality protection, sampling error, nonsampling, and definitions, see Methodology.

Note: Data for 2012 are based on the 2012 NAICS Manual, whereas data for 2007 were based on the 2007 NAICS Manual.

 
  
  Table Name 
  Island Areas: Geographic Area Series: Comparative Statistics by Kind of Business for American Samoa, Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands, Guam, Puerto Rico, and U.S. Virgin Islands: 2012 and 2007 
  
  
  Release Schedule 
  The data in this file are scheduled for release on a flow basis beginning April 2014 through September 2015. 
  
  
  Key Table Information 
  Refer to Survey Methodology for additional information. 
  
  
  Universe 
  The universe includes all establishments with payroll at any time during 2012 and 2007, and classified in NAICS sectors 21-81, with exceptions for: 
    
    American Samoa (Includes establishments without annual payroll only for the 2007 census.) 
    Puerto Rico (Excludes NAICS sectors 23 and 31-33.)  
    Data for 2012 are based on the 2012 NAICS Manual, whereas data for 2007 were based on the 2007 NAICS Manual. 
  
  
  Geography Coverage 
   The data are shown at the state-equivalent level for: 
    
     American Samoa 
    Guam 
    Northern Mariana Islands 
    Puerto Rico 
    U.S. Virgin Islands 
    
  
  
  Industry Coverage 
  For American Samoa, Guam, Northern Mariana Islands, and the U.S. Virgin Islands, the data for 2012 and 2007 are shown for: 
    
    Total of all sectors (00) level 
    Selected 2-digit NAICS code level 
    For Puerto Rico, the data for 2012 and 2007 are shown for: 
    
    Wholesale, retail, and selected kinds of business 
    Selected 2- through 3-digit NAICS code levels 
    
 
  
  Data Items and Other Identifying Records 
  This file contains data for: 
    
    Number of establishments 
    Sales, receipts, revenue, or shipments 
    Annual payroll 
    Number of paid employees 
    
  
  
  Sort Order 
  The data are presented in ascending NAICS code by year sequence.
